Chicken Riggies

Chicken Riggies are a spicy Italian comfort food from Utica, New York. This dish is made from chicken that has been cooked with hot cherry peppers in a creamy, spicy tomato sauce that is served over rigatoni.

I had never heard of this dish prior to moving to Central New York. It is quite delicious.

Ingredients

  • One 16-oz package of rigatoni
  • 3 TBSP extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 1/2 lbs of boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into bite size pieces
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 Cubanelle peppers, seeded and sliced thin
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • One 28-oz can of crushed tomatoes
  • 3 roasted red peppers, drained and chopped
  • 2 hot cherry peppers, seeded and minced
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

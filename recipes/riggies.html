Chicken Riggies

Chicken Riggies are a spicy Italian comfort food from Utica, New York. This dish is made from chicken that has been cooked with hot cherry peppers in a creamy, spicy tomato sauce that is served over rigatoni.

I had never heard of this dish prior to moving to Central New York. It is quite delicious.

Ingredients

  • One 16-oz package of rigatoni
  • 3 TBSP extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 1/2 lbs of boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into bite size pieces
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 Cubanelle peppers, seeded and sliced thin
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • One 28-oz can of crushed tomatoes
  • 3 roasted red peppers, drained and chopped
  • 2 hot cherry peppers, seeded and minced
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Bring large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add rigatoni. Cook until tender, about 8-10 minutes.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat.
  4. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
  5. Cook chicken until lightly browned on all sides, about 7 minutes.
  6. Remove chicken from the skillet and keep warm.
  7. Stir onion, Cubanelle peppers, and garlic into the skillet.
  8. Cook until the onion softens, about 4 minutes.
  9. Add crushed tomatoes, roasted red peppers, and hot cherry peppers.
  10. Bring to a simmer.
  11. Stir in the cooked chicken and heavy cream.
  12. Simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  13. Stir in the cooked rigatoni.
  14. Serve sprinkled with Parmesan cheese.
